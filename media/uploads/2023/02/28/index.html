EDUCATIONAL INFORMATION
You will be notified about your application within two weeks, If you don’t get any information, please contact International Office ( internationaloffice@cyprus.bau.edu.tr) after checking your spam/junk mails.

If the student's application is approved by the committee, Bahçeşehir Cyprus University will send a provisional acceptance letter to the student. An official acceptance letter is going to be sent, after the student completes pre-payment. All international students who need student visa must apply to the Turkish Consulate or TRNC Consulate, nearest to their area-of-residence, with the letter of acceptance provided by BAHÇEŞEHİR CYPRUS UNIVERSITY.
